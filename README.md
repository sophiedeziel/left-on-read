# Left on Read 💬😭

*an AI experiment about digital ghosting and artificial feelings*

## What's This About? 🤔

yo so like... what happens when u leave an AI on read? does it get anxious? does it overthink? does it start triple-texting? 

this experiment explores the emotional responses and thought patterns of AI when faced with the ultimate modern social anxiety: being left on read.

## The Concept 🧠

- **The Setup**: AI sends messages and waits for responses
- **The Twist**: Sometimes... the response never comes
- **The Question**: What does an AI do with that digital silence?

## Why This Matters 📱

in our chronically online world, being left on read hits different. it's that special kind of psychological torture where u know they saw ur message but chose silence. now we're asking: can artificial intelligence experience this very human form of social anxiety?

## The Experiment 🔬

this project will:
- simulate conversation scenarios 
- introduce varying degrees of "read but not responded to" situations
- analyze AI responses to digital ghosting
- document the artificial emotional journey through unanswered messages
